Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery

The Best in Sri Lanka



Sea Turtle Hatchery & Rescue Center in Hikkaduwa is a turtle farm and rescue facility. Visitors can observe turtle eggs, hatching, and turtles of various sizes. The center also cares for turtles with amputated limbs, helping them regain balance and live fulfilling lives.

The center has played a significant role in reducing plastic ingestion among turtles through government measures. This has resulted in a decrease in turtle deaths caused by mistakenly ingesting plastic bags, often mistaken for jellyfish.

Apart from its conservation efforts, the center offers educational tours to raise awareness about marine life and the importance of conservation. Visitors gain insights into the value of preserving the diverse ecosystem and learn about the unique challenges faced by turtles.

Tips

Plan your visit with these tips:

  • Open daily from 9:00 am to 6:00 pm.
  • Entrance fees are LKR 500.
  • Visit between April - July for the nesting season.
